Home Is.In is an online project based off my family's concepts of home. It is meant to be a reading experience that expresses the dispersed, entangled, yet somehow unified manner of our interactions.

Different perspectives are apparent by shifting the location and dialogue in the browser as a form of a hyperlinked novel. Underlined words are key themes that
run through our discussions, and colors signify change in speaker.
